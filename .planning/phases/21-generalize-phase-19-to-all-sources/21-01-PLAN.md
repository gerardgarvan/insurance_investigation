<objective>
Create R/20_all_source_missingness.R -- a standalone diagnostic script that extends Phase 19's UFH-specific payer missingness investigation to ALL partner sites (AMS, UMI, FLM, VRT, UFH) in the HL cohort. Produces 6 combined CSVs (5 breakdowns + 1 cross-site summary) with SOURCE as a grouping dimension, plus console output for quick HiPerGator review.

Purpose: Enable head-to-head comparison of payer data completeness across all five OneFlorida+ partner sites to identify which sites have systematic data submission gaps.

Output: R/20_all_source_missingness.R (standalone script), 6 CSV files in output/tables/ with `all_source_` prefix
</objective>
