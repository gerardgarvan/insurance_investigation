<objective>
Create `R/105_normalize_supportive_care_meaning.R`, which resolves each of the 171
RXNORM codes on the **Supportive Care** tab of
`data/reference/all_codes_resolved_next_tables_v2.1.xlsx` to its **generic ingredient
name** (RxNorm IN concept) and appends a new trailing column `Normalized Meaning`
(column G) to that tab IN PLACE.

canonical ingredient (D-01/D-05/D-06), enabling per-ingredient rollups. Redundancy is
heavy: ~8+ `ondansetron` variants, multiple `dexamethasone` / `dexamethasone phosphate`
variants, brand `Zofran`.

- `data/reference/rxnorm_ingredient_cache.csv` (new bundled cache — offline source of truth)

- `data/reference/all_codes_resolved_next_tables_v2.1.xlsx` (Supportive Care tab gains col G)
