<objective>
Create R/59_death_date_validation.R that validates death dates against treatment timelines, flags post-death clinical activity, investigates death-only patients, and produces a three-sheet xlsx report plus validated_death_dates.rds artifact.

Purpose: Death dates in PCORnet data may contain impossible values (death before treatment), post-death clinical activity (data entry errors or delayed reporting), and death-only patients with no treatment context. This script identifies all these data quality issues for manual review and produces a cleaned death dates artifact for downstream Gantt export.

Output: R/59_death_date_validation.R, output/death_date_validation.xlsx (3 sheets), output/death_date_validation.csv, validated_death_dates.rds
