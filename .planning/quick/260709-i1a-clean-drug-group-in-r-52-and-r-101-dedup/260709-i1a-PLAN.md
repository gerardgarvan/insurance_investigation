<objective>
Clean the `drug_group` multi-value Gantt column so it is deduped, sorted, semicolon-separated, and free of literal "NA" tokens — matching every other multi-value column. Currently drug_group appears as comma-separated with duplicates and literal "NA" (e.g. "SCT,SCT,SCT", "Chemotherapy,NA", "NA,NA") in both output/gantt_episodes.csv (R/52) and output/gantt_lifespan.csv (R/101).

Purpose: drug_group is the sole multi-value column skipping cleanup, breaking downstream consistency. Two root causes: (1) R/52 never calls clean_multi_value() on drug_group; (2) clean_multi_value() strips "" and R-NA but not the literal string "NA"; plus (3) R/101's union_field() only splits on ";" so it cannot dedup drug_group's comma-delimited legacy values.

Output: Modified R/52, R/101, R/88 — structural (grep/parse) verification only, no pipeline run.
</objective>
